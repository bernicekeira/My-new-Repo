eMobilis Tech Institute

Our history

eMobilis was started in the year 2010. it had first begun with 15 students who had the passion to know technology. it first provided computer packages and lately got the idea of creating more opportunities for students. As years went by the number of students in the institution increased and was later approved by the ministry of education having successfully given over 100 students graduating.

What do we do

Proving Quality Education

The institute provides all computer packages and computer skills that hereby nurture the students with great computer skills. Here the students can acquire jobs in different fields in the employment sector.

Nurturing Talents

All students get to establish new talents and grow mentally, physically and economically. Skills like coding, elementary programming and others and most learnt skills that students. some students attempt to create gadgets that help th human society.

Character and Moral Values

Character and morals are highly valued in the institution and each student must display a great character and must have good morals. Participation of good conduct and morals is usually appreciated by the institution staff and the students is congratulated for the great discipline.
